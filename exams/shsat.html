What is the SHSAT?

The Specialized High School Admissions Test (SHSAT) is for students in grades 8 or 9 who wish to apply to New York City's specialized high school.

Along with the test, students will also submit an application listing their preferred choice of schools.

A student's score on this test and their preferred choice will determine their admission into a particular specialized high school.
